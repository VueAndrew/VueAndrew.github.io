The Dodge Challenger SRT lineup includes the Hellcat, Hellcat Redeye, and Super Stock trims. The Hellcat has a supercharged 6.2-liter HEMI V8 engine, while the Redeye variant offers even more power. The Super Stock trim is designed for drag racing, featuring specialized components for enhanced acceleration and track performance. Each trim provides a distinct driving experience tailored to enthusiasts seeking top-tier performance and power.

Nickname

The Dodge Challenger SRT Hellcat Widebody earned the nickname "boat" due to its substantial size and weight. Some car enthusiasts and critics have jokingly referred to it as a "boat" due to its wide and heavy build, which gives it a certain presence on the road. Despite the humorous nickname, the Hellcat Widebody is renowned for its impressive power and performance, making it a favorite among muscle car enthusiasts.
